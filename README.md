# General Accountant Automation System

> **A Python-based system for automating accounting workflows, including financial record analysis, bank reconciliations, journal entries, month-end close processes, internal audits, and payment discrepancy resolutions. Ensures adherence to regulatory standards and improves account accuracy.**

---

##  **Repository Overview**

### **Purpose**
This system is designed to **streamline and automate** the core responsibilities of an **Accountant**, including:
- **Financial record analysis** to uncover discrepancies.
- **Bank reconciliation** for cash accounts.
- **Journal entry preparation and posting**.
- **Month-end close processes** (accruals, prepayments, allocations).
- **Internal audits** to ensure compliance with policies and procedures.
- **Payment discrepancy investigations** for incoming and outgoing transactions.

### **Key Achievements**
- **Discrepancy Detection**: Automatically flags mismatches in financial records.
- **Efficiency**: Reduces manual effort in reconciliation and month-end close tasks.
- **Compliance**: Ensures adherence to regulatory standards and internal policies.
- **Accuracy**: Improves account accuracy through automated validation and auditing.

---

##  **Features**
   Feature                     | Description                                                                                     |
 |-----------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
 | **Financial Records**       | Create, track, and manage financial records (revenues, expenses, assets, liabilities).       |
 | **Bank Reconciliation**     | Automate reconciliation of book balances with bank statements.                                |
 | **Journal Entries**         | Create and validate balanced journal entries (debits = credits).                             |
 | **Month-End Close**         | Manage accruals, prepayments, allocations, and journal entries for month-end adjustments.      |
 | **Internal Audits**         | Conduct audits, log findings, and assign corrective actions.                                   |
 | **Payment Discrepancies**   | Investigate and resolve discrepancies in incoming/outgoing payments.                          |
 | **Financial Analysis**      | Generate reports and detect discrepancies in financial records.                             |
